Smart India Hackathon

A Nationwide Movement to Foster Innovation

About SIH

Smart India Hackathon is a nationwide initiative by the Ministry of Education's Innovation Cell. It's designed to provide students with a platform to solve some of the pressing problems we face in our daily lives, and thus inculcate a culture of product innovation and a mindset of problem-solving.

It has grown into the world's biggest open innovation model, offering a unique opportunity for students to work on real-world challenges posed by the Government, private sector organizations, and NGOs.

Achievements

Over the years, SIH has seen millions of students participate. Many of the solutions developed during the hackathon have been implemented by various ministries and private organizations, leading to:

  • Improved digital infrastructure for government services.
  • Innovative solutions for agriculture, healthcare, and education.
  • A significant boost to the startup ecosystem.

Placements & Opportunities

SIH is not just a competition; it's a gateway to incredible opportunities. Top-performing teams and individuals often receive:

  • Direct placement interviews with leading tech companies.
  • Internship opportunities at prestigious organizations.
  • Seed funding and incubation support to turn their projects into real startups.
